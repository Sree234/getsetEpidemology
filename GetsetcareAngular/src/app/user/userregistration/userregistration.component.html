Get Set Epidemology (Integrated Medical Management System) is a revolutionary application that fills the gap between Doctors,Hospitals Paramedics , Lab technicians, Pharmacitsts and Patients. It's Advanced EMR(Electronic Medical Records) gets rid of paper from a doctor's office. It synchronises with the patient's personal health assistant system irrespective of health care service center(public or private) Therefore it prevents loss or duplication of patients health care data and provides accurate health care census. It's unified cloud architecture will allow organizations to work together and enables to deliver healthcare services faster, smarter, and more convenient.
